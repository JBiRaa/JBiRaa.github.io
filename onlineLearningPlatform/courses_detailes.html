Interactive English Program

Bendahane Abdljabar

The Interactive English Program is a free program offered by Edraak and has been specially developed to meet the needs and interests of Arabic speaking students. This program will help you learn English and build and develop your overall skills, including reading, writing, listening and speaking, as well as communication and interpersonal skills.
